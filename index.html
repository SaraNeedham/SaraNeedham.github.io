Don Bugito

Insects as an Alternative to Meat?

Monica Martinez, owner of Don Bugito 'Prehispanic Snackeria' in San Francisco, takes us into her kitchen to show us how she makes snacks made of California-farmed edible insects. Based on Pre-Columbian Mexican cuisine, her creations reveal how insects can be a tasty and sustainable protein alternative to meat.

Percentage Change of Land Devoted to Ag

Food Waste

Food Wasted by Type

Approximately 40% of the U.S. food supply is thrown out. Here's a percentage breakdown of food wasted by type. Not surprising is the high percentage of perishable food, like fresh fruits and fish. But, it's interesting to see the high percentage of sugar and grain; items considered to have a much longer shelf life.
